About Website

After decades of struggles within the panda population, Pandas would no
longer be classified as endangered species.
While this seems to be a positive step forward in the right direction,
this doesn't mean that the struggle is over.

Problems:

Pandas have been facing various struggles in maintaining the population.
although they would no longer be classified as endangered species according
to the IUCN red list's 2016 assessment in April 11, the struggles are still far from over.

Our goals:

Our goals are to spread awareness, and to encourage
visitors of this page(such as your self) to take action in helping the
panda population.

Your roles:

In regards to our goals, you can contribute in many ways!
some of these include sharing this page with others,
and making donations to other organizations.
